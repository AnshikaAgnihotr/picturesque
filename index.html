About Picturesque

Picturesque is a new brand that is a place for photoholic, photogenic, and photography lover. It is a photography studio that offers the clients a aesthetic vibes and place to get their dreamy pictures. The studio organizes several photography contests also the brand conducts workshops in different cities. The studio deals in different types of photography like portrait photography, landscape photography, fashion photography, product photography, fine art photography, event photography, sports photography, food photography, macro photography, artificial background photography and many more and the brand also collaborates with some travel agencies for travel photography. The studio provides an opportunity for photography enthusiasts to develop their interest in different types of photography, as well as knowledge about photography props and editing. This studio also gives a chance to some people who have great knowledge about photography but due to some compulsions they are not able to move forward in their desired field.
